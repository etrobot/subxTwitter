18 Jan 2024 21:05:44 @swyx: Flow engineering is also why:

  • OpenAI is probably correct to deprecate completion over chat for majority of users
  • multiturn instruction tuning is the next frontier for VLMs (watch the LLaVA family)
  • (for now) developer inner loop agents (Codium, Cursor, Cody, et al) beat outer loop agents (too hands off). analogous to Level 1/2 vs Level 3/4/5 self driving problems.

As much as the norm is K-shot prompting, we will need to evolve new standards for L-turn flows... (aka "chains" :) )

...and deal with the less deterministic, hard to reproduce, but overwhelmingly better results.

In the @latentspacepod paper club yesterday we coincidentally mentioned "The Impact of Reasoning Step Length on Large Language Models" (arxiv.org/abs/2401.04925), aka "more steps is all you need"

*all the above are general statements, local- and domain-specific exceptions abound
