On 05 Jan 2024, @ShepJoel tweeted about the 30-year-old prediction they made as a child, regarding China's economic development. They highlighted the shift from labor-intensive manufacturing to value-added manufacturing and services, as well as the difficulty an autocratic nation faces in running a modern technology and services economy. They suggested that China would eventually have to choose between continued high growth or autocratic control, with the latter ultimately being chosen.

This tweet was in response to another tweet discussing the uncertainty of future costs in predicting the technology mix of a least-cost zero-emission electricity system.

In another tweet, @KenCaldeira shared the difficulty in predicting a least-cost zero-emission electricity system due to uncertain future costs.

In the realm of politics, @ShanuMathew93 praised the work of @JigarShahDC and their team in transforming an office into a powerhouse for innovation and funding while restoring its reputation.

More specific to China, a tweet by @SCMPNews discussed Hong Kong's relationship with Beijing, disputing the notion that Beijing is turning Hong Kong into just another Chinese city.

The Twitter sphere also dabbled into philosophical inquiries, with a tweet by @gojomo, posing the question of being a "correlated asset versus an uncorrelated asset," adding a touch of philosophical depth.

The diverse range of topics on Twitter is demonstrated through a tweet by @amplifiedamp discussing "Yud's e/acc arc," and another tweet by @bryan_caplan discussing a defense of conformism by @IlyaSomin.

The impact of diverse attitudes and beliefs on social issues was also a point of discussion, as highlighted in a tweet by @Scholars_Stage exploring the reasons behind opposing abortion.

Rounding off the round-up, the Twitter conversation turned into a lighter note with a tweet by @gojomo humorously musing about impersonators of skateboarding legend Tony Hawk.

As the world continues to evolve and diversify, Twitter serves as a platform for a wide range of discussions spanning from economic predictions to philosophical ponderings. Stay tuned for more insights and debates from the Twitter sphere.
